## Blockchains & Protocols

- [Bitcoin](https://bitcoin.org) – The original decentralized peer-to-peer monetary network and store-of-value protocol.
- [Ethereum](https://ethereum.org) – The leading smart-contract platform powering DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, and L2 scaling ecosystems.
- [Solana](https://solana.com) – High-throughput chain optimized for fast finality, low fees, and consumer-grade apps.
- [Cardano](https://cardano.org) – Research-driven proof-of-stake chain with a strong focus on formal methods and governance.
- [Polkadot](https://polkadot.network) – Interoperability protocol enabling specialized chains (parachains) to share security and messaging.
- [Avalanche](https://avax.network) – Multi-network architecture (subnets) designed for custom chains and rapid transaction finality.
- [Cosmos](https://cosmos.network) – Interchain ecosystem built around IBC for secure cross-chain communication.
- [NEAR](https://near.org) – Developer-friendly chain with sharding and account abstractions aimed at mainstream onboarding.
- [Tezos](https://tezos.com) – Self-amending chain with on-chain governance and a long history of protocol upgrades.
- [Algorand](https://www.algorand.foundation) – Pure proof-of-stake chain focused on fast finality, low fees, and institutional use.
- [Stellar](https://www.stellar.org) – Payments-oriented network designed for fast cross-border transfers and token issuance.
- [XRPL](https://xrpl.org) – Ledger optimized for payments and tokenization with built-in DEX functionality.
- [Tron](https://tron.network) – High-activity chain widely used for stablecoin transfers and consumer crypto apps.
- [Aptos](https://aptosfoundation.org) – Move-based L1 emphasizing performance, safety, and modern smart-contract design.
- [Sui](https://sui.io) – Object-centric Move chain built for high performance and scalable on-chain assets.
- [BNB Chain](https://www.bnbchain.org) – EVM ecosystem with wide adoption for DeFi and high-frequency retail usage.
- [Hedera](https://hedera.com) – Hashgraph-based public network with enterprise governance and strong throughput claims.
- [IOTA](https://www.iota.org) – DAG-oriented network focused on IoT and feeless transfers in certain architectures.
